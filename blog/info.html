Best Hotels in Koh Samui

Whether you’re seeking barefoot luxury, elegant resorts, or hidden gems, Koh Samui offers a range of accommodations to fit every traveler’s taste.

Luxury Picks

Four Seasons Resort Koh Samui — Nestled on a private hillside, this five‑star sanctuary offers infinity pools, private villas, and legendary service with breathtaking views of the Gulf of Thailand.
The Ritz‑Carlton Koh Samui — A blend of modern luxury and Thai tradition, with a private bay, spa village, and curated experiences for guests seeking top‑tier island living.
Banyan Tree Samui — Hillside pool villas with panoramic sea views and a holistic spa program make this a favorite for wellness‑minded travelers.

High‑End / Normal Resorts

SALA Samui Chaweng Beach Resort — Stylish beachfront retreat with pool suites and rooftop dining overlooking Chaweng’s turquoise waters.
Hansar Samui Resort — Steps from Fisherman’s Village; chic design, beachside pool, and walkable dining.
Rocky’s Boutique Resort — Intimate, family‑run escape near Lamai with warm service and a quiet cove setting.

Wild Cards

Treehouse Villas Koh Samui — Eco‑leaning, treehouse‑style stays for travelers seeking something truly different.
The Jungle Club — Rustic hillside bungalows with one of the island’s best panoramas.

Top Restaurants in Koh Samui

From Michelin‑rated fine dining to beachfront Thai kitchens and quirky cafés, Koh Samui’s food scene is as diverse as it is delicious.

Luxury Dining

Dining on the Rocks — Six Senses Samui — Iconic clifftop venue serving contemporary tasting menus with sweeping ocean views.
Tree Tops Sky Dining & Bar — Private treetop pods and refined multi‑course menus in a dramatic setting.
The Cliff Bar & Grill — Mediterranean‑leaning plates and a clifftop terrace over the gulf.

High‑End / Normal

Fisherman’s Village Restaurants — A walkable strip of seaside eateries from Thai seafood grills to international bistros.
Red Snapper — Stylish Chaweng mainstay with fresh seafood and occasional live jazz.
Coco Tam’s — Sand‑side bar/restaurant for wood‑fired pizzas, sunsets, and fire shows.

Wild Cards

Noori India — Long‑running local favorite for authentic North Indian flavors.
The Hungry Wolf — Big‑hearted burgers and comfort food in Lamai.

Best Beaches in Koh Samui

The coastline ranges from lively stretches with nightlife to quiet coves ideal for retreat‑style downtime.

Luxury Beaches

Chaweng Beach — Samui’s most famous strip with luxury resorts, dining, and nightlife.
Lamai Beach — A touch quieter than Chaweng with a broad mix of stays and family‑friendly vibes.
Bophut Beach — Refined, boutique energy anchored by Fisherman’s Village.

High‑End / Normal

Silver Beach (Thongtakian) — Small, pretty cove with calm, swim‑friendly waters.
Choeng Mon Beach — Peaceful bay favored by couples and families, away from the bustle.
Maenam Beach — Long golden arc with a laid‑back, barefoot vibe.

Wild Cards

Coral Cove — Pocket‑sized bay with clear water and easy snorkeling.
Taling Ngam Beach — West‑coast sunsets and views to the Five Islands; serene and uncrowded.

Sports & Fitness in Koh Samui

Wellness is core to the island’s identity—from world‑class yoga and spa retreats to Muay Thai camps and dive schools.

Luxury Fitness & Wellness

Kamalaya Wellness Sanctuary — Globally acclaimed for holistic programs spanning detox, stress relief, and integrative medicine.
Absolute Sanctuary — Yoga, Pilates, and fitness intensives with nutrition‑forward dining.
Vikasa Yoga Retreat — Ocean‑view yoga shalas, teacher trainings, and wellness café culture.

High‑End / Normal

Superpro Samui — One of Thailand’s best‑known Muay Thai/MMA camps, welcoming all levels.
Samui Fitness Retreat — Bootcamp‑style programs for conditioning, strength, and weight loss.
Fitkoh — Transformation‑focused schedules combining training, nutrition, and recovery.

Wild Cards

CrossFit Lamai — Dedicated box in Lamai for serious strength and community WODs.
Easy Divers Samui — PADI dive school for fitness underwater—intro dives to pro certs.

Experience Koh Samui — and Invest in Its Future

Koh Samui is more than a destination—it’s a lifestyle. Whether you’re drawn by luxury resorts, authentic dining, or world‑class wellness, the island offers something for everyone. With strong demand and finite island supply, investing here has never been more promising.
