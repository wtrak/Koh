Best Hotels in Koh Samui

Whether you’re seeking barefoot luxury, elegant resorts, or hidden gems, Koh Samui offers a range of accommodations to fit every traveler’s taste.

Luxury Picks

  • [Four Seasons Resort Koh Samui](https://www.fourseasons.com/kohsamui/) – Nestled on a private hillside, this five-star sanctuary offers infinity pools, private villas, and legendary service with breathtaking views of the Gulf of Thailand.
  • [The Ritz-Carlton Koh Samui](https://www.ritzcarlton.com/en/hotels/koh-samui) – A blend of modern luxury and Thai tradition, offering a private bay, spa village, and curated experiences for guests seeking the best in island living.
  • [Banyan Tree Samui](https://www.banyantree.com/thailand/samui) – Known for its hillside pool villas and holistic wellness focus, this resort combines panoramic sea views with world-class spa treatments.

High-End / Normal Resorts

  • [Sala Samui Chaweng Beach Resort](https://www.salahospitality.com/samui-chaweng/) – A stylish beachfront retreat with pool suites and rooftop dining overlooking Chaweng Beach.
  • [Hansar Samui Resort](https://www.hansarsamui.com/) – Located in Fisherman’s Village, Hansar offers chic modern design with a laid-back beachfront vibe.
  • [Rocky’s Boutique Resort](https://www.rockyresort.com/) – A romantic, family-run escape near Lamai Beach that combines personal service with intimate luxury.

Wild Cards

  • [Treehouse Villas Koh Samui](https://www.booking.com/hotel/th/treehouse-villas-samui.html) – Eco-friendly treehouse-style accommodations perfect for travelers seeking something different and adventurous.
  • [The Jungle Club](https://www.jungleclubsamui.com/) – Perched high in the hills, this rustic retreat offers simple bungalows with the best panoramic views of Koh Samui.

Top Restaurants in Koh Samui

From Michelin-rated fine dining to beachfront Thai kitchens and quirky cafés, Koh Samui’s food scene is as diverse as it is delicious.

Luxury Dining

  • [Dining on the Rocks – Six Senses Samui](https://www.sixsenses.com/en/resorts/samui/dining/dining-on-the-rocks) – Iconic clifftop restaurant serving contemporary cuisine with sweeping ocean views.
  • [Tree Tops Sky Dining & Bar](https://www.anantara.com/en/bophut-koh-samui/restaurants/tree-tops-sky-dining) – Private dining pods perched in the treetops, offering gourmet tasting menus in an unforgettable setting.
  • [The Cliff Bar & Grill](https://www.thecliffsamui.com/) – Known for its Mediterranean-inspired menu and oceanfront clifftop views.

High-End / Normal

  • [Fisherman’s Village Restaurants](https://www.kosamui.com/fishermans-village/) – A collection of seaside restaurants ranging from Thai seafood grills to international bistros.
  • [Red Snapper](https://www.facebook.com/redsnapper.samui/) – A stylish Chaweng restaurant with a focus on fresh seafood and live jazz music.
  • [Coco Tam’s](https://www.cocotams.com/) – Famous beach bar and restaurant where beanbags on the sand meet wood-fired pizzas and fire shows.

Wild Cards

  • [Noori India](https://www.tripadvisor.com/Restaurant_Review-g293918-d1148083-Reviews-Noori_India-Ko_Samui_Surat_Thani_Province.html) – One of the island’s most authentic Indian restaurants, a surprise hit among both tourists and expats.
  • [The Hungry Wolf](https://www.thehungrywolfkohsamui.com/) – A quirky Lamai spot famous for its massive burgers and comfort food.

Best Beaches in Koh Samui

Koh Samui’s coastline offers everything from lively stretches filled with nightlife to quiet coves ideal for relaxation.

Luxury Beaches

  • [Chaweng Beach](https://www.kosamui.com/chaweng/) – The island’s most famous beach, lined with luxury resorts, nightlife, and a vibrant atmosphere.
  • [Lamai Beach](https://www.kosamui.com/lamai/) – A slightly quieter option than Chaweng, with a mix of high-end resorts and family-friendly vibes.
  • [Bophut Beach](https://www.kosamui.com/bophut/) – Home to Fisherman’s Village, offering a more refined, boutique beach experience.

High-End / Normal

  • [Silver Beach (Thongtakian)](https://www.kosamui.com/magazine/silver-beach.htm) – Small but stunning, with calm waters perfect for swimming.
  • [Choeng Mon Beach](https://www.kosamui.com/choeng-mon/) – A peaceful beach often favored by families and couples looking to avoid the crowds.
  • [Maenam Beach](https://www.kosamui.com/maenam/) – Known for its laid-back atmosphere and long golden stretch of sand.

Wild Cards

  • [Coral Cove](https://www.tripadvisor.com/Attraction_Review-g293918-d447270-Reviews-Coral_Cove_Beach-Ko_Samui_Surat_Thani_Province.html) – A small, hidden bay with crystal-clear water, great for snorkeling.
  • [Taling Ngam Beach](https://www.kosamui.com/taling-ngam/) – A serene escape offering incredible sunsets and views of the Five Islands.

Sports & Fitness in Koh Samui

Wellness and fitness are at the heart of Koh Samui’s lifestyle. From world-class gyms and yoga retreats to Muay Thai camps, the island is a hub for active travelers.

Luxury Fitness & Wellness

  • [Kamalaya Wellness Sanctuary](https://www.kamalaya.com/) – A globally acclaimed wellness retreat focusing on holistic health, yoga, and spa therapies.
  • [Absolute Sanctuary](https://www.absolutesanctuary.com/) – Specializes in yoga, detox, and fitness programs with a resort-like atmosphere.
  • [Vikasa Yoga Retreat](https://vikasayoga.com/) – Famous for yoga teacher training and retreats overlooking the ocean.

High-End / Normal

  • [Superpro Samui](https://www.superprosamui.com/) – Thailand’s leading Muay Thai and MMA training camp, popular with beginners and pros.
  • [Samui Fitness Retreat](https://samuifitnessretreat.com/) – A bootcamp-style retreat for strength, conditioning, and weight loss.
  • [Fitkoh](https://fitkoh.com/) – Focused on fitness transformation programs, combining exercise, diet, and community.

Wild Cards

  • [CrossFit Lamai](https://www.facebook.com/crossfitlamai/) – A dedicated CrossFit box in the heart of Lamai, perfect for strength enthusiasts.
  • [Easy Divers Samui](https://www.easydivers-samui.com/) – For those who prefer their fitness underwater, this PADI dive school offers incredible scuba experiences.
